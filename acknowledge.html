Acknowledge SMARTS

Referencing SMARTS In Publications

There is no specific wording for acknowledgements to SMARTS, but we do ask that you contact Victoria Misenti so we may display your publication on our webpage. Please also include the words "operated by the SMARTS Consortium" in addition to the specific telescope/instrument combination when describing your observations. When appropriate, a reference to SMARTS in the abstract of papers is appreciated.
